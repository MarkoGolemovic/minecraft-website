LEGACY

Despite this game being old today it is still a huge inspiration for other games that are currently being created.
Its huge success led to other developers wanting in on the huge market causing them to start developing more and more sandbox survival games.

The most notable example would be the game Terraria. It is a 2D survival and crafting sandbox in which you gather materials, mine, and explore a randomly generated map. You can also interact with a variety of NPCs, have a social life, trade and sell and even get married!
