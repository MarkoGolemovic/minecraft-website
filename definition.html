MINECRAFT

Minecraft is an open world, randomly generated, sandbox construction game for the PC, XBox, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch, and mobile phones.
It was created on May 10, 2009, and has been updating ever since. The official release date of the full, paid version is November 18, 2011. Its creator and founder of Mojang AB, Markus “Notch” Persson, created Minecraft inspired by the games Infiniminer, Dwarf Fortress, Dungeon Keeper, and some of his own previous games, Legend of the Chambered and RubyDung.

Minecraft’s main gimmick is the fact that the entire world is made of 3D blocks. Simple square or rectangular shapes, with pixel textures. Players can place and break these blocks to create structures and art, or play in a survival mode of gathering resources and fighting off monsters while building, farming, and interacting with NPCs.
The game is also available in multiplayer, where players can connect and build together, PvP, or cooperate to survive and reach the end.
With over 30 million PC copies, and 154 million copies of any version sold worldwide, Minecraft is the second best selling videogame of all time.
